PlayStation Plus users are in for a treat with the addition of a unique indie title called The Exit 8, a free puzzle game that has garnered attention for its eerie atmosphere and engaging gameplay. This game, developed by Kotake Create, invites players to navigate a hauntingly looped Japanese metro tunnel while identifying various anomalies that challenge their perception and critical thinking skills.

While The Exit 8 is not strictly a horror game, it masterfully incorporates unsettling elements that can leave players feeling unnerved. The gameplay revolves around spotting discrepancies in the environment, from harmless details to spine-chilling surprises. Completing the game requires players to identify these anomalies accurately over eight loops, making it a short yet memorable experience that can be completed in just a couple of hours.

With easy trophy achievements and a compelling mix of obvious and subtle anomalies, The Exit 8 is a perfect addition to the PlayStation Plus library.
