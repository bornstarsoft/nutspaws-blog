Exciting news for gaming enthusiasts! The popular multiplayer party game, Fruit Mountain Party, is set to launch on both the Switch and Switch 2 in Spring 2026. Currently available in Early Access on PC, this vibrant 3D puzzle game has captured the attention of players with its unique fruit-combining mechanics and engaging multiplayer modes.

Players can look forward to a thrilling experience with various versus modes that promise both competitive and chaotic gameplay. The Switch version will be showcased at upcoming events, including Osaka Game Dungeon and Tokyo eSports Festa 2026, giving fans a sneak peek at what to expect. With features like online battles and a single-player ranking system, Fruit Mountain Party aims to deliver fun for everyone.

As we await its release, one can't help but wonder how this game will evolve on the Switch platform. Will it bring a fresh wave of multiplayer fun to the console? Only time will tell!
