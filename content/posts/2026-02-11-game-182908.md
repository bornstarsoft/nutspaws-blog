Apple has exciting news for gamers as it announces the arrival of four new games to Apple Arcade in March, including the highly anticipated "Oceanhorn 3: Legend of the Shadow Sea." Launching on March 5, this action-RPG-adventure continues the beloved franchise nearly a thousand years after its predecessor, inviting players to explore enchanting islands and battle titans in a beautifully crafted 3D world.

In addition to Oceanhorn 3, players can look forward to engaging titles like "Pocket Love!+" where you can create a cozy home with your partner, "Flow Free+" for puzzle enthusiasts, and "Doraemon Dorayaki Shop Story+" for fans of shop management games. All these titles will be available on iPhone and iPad, making it easier than ever to enjoy gaming on the go.
