Mario Kart World has just rolled out an exciting update that introduces a brand new battle mode, Bob-omb Blast, which allows players to hold up to 10 bombs and strategically throw them based on how long they press the L button. This addition is sure to spice up gameplay and provide a fresh experience for both new and veteran players alike.

In addition to the new battle mode, the update also includes various adjustments to items like Bullet Bill and the Boomerang, along with fixes for issues related to Mega Mushrooms. These tweaks aim to enhance the overall gameplay experience, ensuring that players can enjoy a smoother and more balanced game.
