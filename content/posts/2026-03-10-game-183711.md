If you're a fan of cozy games and adorable cats, then "Kitty Haven" is a title you won't want to miss. This upcoming puzzle game invites players to create the perfect sanctuary for their feline friends, each with their own unique quirks and needs. The challenge lies in arranging the space to accommodate picky little furballs, whether they're greedy, have sensitive noses, or are prone to stubborn cat fights. 

As you progress, you'll unlock new items and residents, allowing you to customize your haven further. The game not only focuses on solving puzzles but also on decorating and creating an ideal environment for your kittens, making it a delightful blend of strategy and creativity. With a demo available now on Steam, players can dive into this charming world and experience the joy of crafting a cozy retreat for their furry companions.

As we look forward to its full release, one can't help but wonder: how will "Kitty Haven" redefine the cozy gaming genre?
