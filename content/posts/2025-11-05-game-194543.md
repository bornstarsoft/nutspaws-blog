November is shaping up to be an exciting month for Xbox Game Pass subscribers, with Microsoft announcing a robust lineup of 11 new titles. Among these, the highly anticipated "Call of Duty: Black Ops 7" will be available for Game Pass Ultimate and PC Game Pass users starting November 14. This addition is sure to excite fans of the franchise and enhance the overall value of the subscription service.

The new titles include a mix of genres, from action-packed adventures like "Dead Static Drive" and "Sniper Elite Resistance" to unique experiences such as "Pigeon Simulator" and "Whiskerwood." With such a diverse selection, players are bound to find something that piques their interest. However, it's important to note that several games will be leaving the service on November 15, including "Blacksmith Master" and "STALKER 2: Heart of Chernobyl," so gamers should make sure to play these before they're gone.
