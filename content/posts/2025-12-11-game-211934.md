This December, PlayStation Plus subscribers are in for a treat with the addition of notable titles like *Assassins Creed Mirage* and *Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty* to the Game Catalog. These games, available for both PS5 and PS4, promise to deliver thrilling gameplay experiences that fans of action and adventure will not want to miss. Additionally, the inclusion of *Soulcalibur 3* in the Classic Catalog adds a nostalgic touch for long-time gamers.

In total, the Game Catalog will expand with a variety of titles on December 16, including *Skate Story*, *Granblue Fantasy: Relink*, and *Planet Coaster 2*. This diverse lineup ensures that theres something for everyone, whether youre into fast-paced action or creative simulation games. The addition of family-friendly titles like *Paw Patrol: Grand Prix* and *Lego Horizon Adventures* also highlights PlayStation's commitment to catering to younger audiences.
