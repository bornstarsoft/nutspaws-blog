In a significant move to broaden its global gaming presence, JetSynthesys has acquired the popular merge puzzle game EverMerge, which has amassed over $350 million in lifetime revenue. This acquisition marks a pivotal step in JetSynthesys' strategy to create a diverse gaming ecosystem that spans multiple genres and markets. With EverMerge's strong performance, particularly in the US and Western Europe, JetSynthesys aims to leverage its expertise in community engagement and user acquisition to introduce the game to emerging markets like India.

Rajan Navani, Chairman & MD of JetSynthesys, highlighted the acquisition as a milestone in recognizing the potential of the orange economy in India. The company plans to enhance EverMerge's appeal through unique cultural storytelling and technology-driven innovations, aiming to resonate with players in new regions. As JetSynthesys continues to invest in EverMerge, the focus will be on expanding content and delivering fresh experiences that keep players engaged.

This acquisition not only reinforces JetSynthesys' commitment to its global player community but also accelerates its journey toward becoming a leading player in the global gaming industry. How will this strategic move shape the future of gaming in emerging markets?
