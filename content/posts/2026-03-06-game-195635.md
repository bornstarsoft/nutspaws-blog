In a stunning recognition of its impact, Battlefield 6 has been awarded both the UK Game of the Year and Best PC Game at the UKIE Video Game Awards 2026. This first-person shooter, developed by Battlefield Studios with contributions from Criterion Games, has not only set a record for the series by selling over seven million copies in its first three days but also emerged as the top-selling game in the American market for 2025.

The awards ceremony highlighted a diverse range of games, with Atomfall taking home the Best UK Console Game award and PowerWash Simulator being recognized as the Best UK Mobile Game. Additionally, the event celebrated significant contributions to the industry, including the induction of four prominent figures into the UK Games Industry Hall of Fame. As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, it will be fascinating to see how these accolades influence future game development and player engagement.
