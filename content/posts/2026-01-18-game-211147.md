In the vast ocean of free games on Steam, some titles shine brighter than others, and one such gem is "12, Memory Lane." Released on December 15, 2025, this indie game has quickly garnered "Very Positive" reviews, proving that you don't need a hefty price tag to deliver a memorable gaming experience. With its engaging narrative and intriguing puzzles, it's a must-play for anyone who appreciates the artistry of indie games.

Set in the eerie estate of the Villemaine family, players embark on a journey to uncover the mystery behind their disappearance, guided by the journals of the late patriarch, Henry Villemaine. While the game is relatively short, clocking in at about an hour and 45 minutes, it offers a rich, emotional experience that resonates long after the credits roll. The developers, a group of students under the name Forget-Me-Not Studio, have impressively crafted a game that rivals more established titles in the genre.

Despite some critiques regarding its controls and length, the overall reception highlights a growing trend: free indie games are not just a novelty but a legitimate force in the gaming industry. As we look to the future, one can't help but wonder what other hidden treasures await in the world of free gaming. Will you add "12, Memory Lane" to your library today?
