Are you eager to dive into the world of Saros? Knowing the exact release times for both the Deluxe and Standard Editions can make all the difference in your gaming experience. The Deluxe Edition offers a 48-hour early access window, allowing dedicated fans to jump in before the general release.

For those who purchased the Deluxe Edition, the game becomes available on April 27 at 9:00 PM Pacific Time, with subsequent times across major cities worldwide. Standard Edition players will have to wait until April 29 to start their adventure. If youve pre-ordered the digital version, pre-loading is already available, ensuring youre ready to play as soon as the clock strikes release time.
