The beloved PS1 shoot 'em up, Sonic Wings Special, is making a triumphant return to modern consoles this week, bringing nostalgia and excitement to both old fans and new players alike. This re-release, part of Hamster Corporation's Console Archives series, allows gamers to experience the thrill of piloting one of 26 fighter ships across 17 stages, all while navigating a unique branching stage selection system.

Originally launched in 1996, Sonic Wings Special is a compilation of the features from the previous Sonic Wings games, known in the West as Aero Fighters. While it had a limited release in Europe, this new version is set to be available on PS5 and Switch 2 for $11.99, making it accessible to a wider audience. With the retro gaming scene thriving, it's exciting to see classic titles like this being revived for modern platforms.

As Hamster continues to expand its Console Archives series, which already includes other gems like Ninja Gaiden 2 and Cool Boarders, fans are left wondering what other hidden treasures might be unearthed next. Will you be picking up Sonic Wings Special to relive the glory days of the PS1 era?
