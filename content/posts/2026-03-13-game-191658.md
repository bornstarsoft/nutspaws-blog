Hazard Pay, the upcoming pixel art block-pushing puzzle game, promises to deliver a unique blend of stealth, survival horror, and narrative-driven gameplay. Set in a Cold War-era research facility, players take on the role of a government cleanup specialist tasked with navigating a world filled with terrifying bio-organisms and moral dilemmas. The games handcrafted puzzle levels and branching narrative choices are designed to keep players engaged while they uncover the dark secrets hidden within the facility.

As you traverse the eerie environments, from laboratories to experimental chambers, you'll face deadly creatures that hunt by scent and sound. The game challenges players not only to survive but also to make impactful decisions that affect the fate of trapped scientists and the outcome of the story. With its focus on environmental storytelling and unique clean-up mechanics, Hazard Pay is set to redefine the puzzle genre in gaming.

With its launch on PS5, Switch, and PC just around the corner, fans of indie games and puzzle enthusiasts alike are eagerly anticipating the chance to dive into this chilling narrative. Will you be the one to expose the horrors within, or will you become just another disposable tool of the system?
