In a surprising turn of events, Ubisoft's latest installment in the Assassin's Creed franchise, titled *Assassin's Creed Shadows*, has emerged as the best-selling console game of 2025, excluding sports titles and shooters. Despite facing a wave of online criticism, this game has captivated a significant audience, proving that engaging gameplay can often overshadow negative discourse.

According to a recent report by industry analysts at Newzoo, *Assassin's Creed Shadows* has managed to secure its position at the top of the sales charts, even amidst fierce competition from established franchises like Electronic Arts' sports titles and the Battlefield series. This remarkable achievement highlights the game's ability to resonate with players, showcasing the enduring appeal of the Assassin's Creed brand.

As we look ahead, it will be interesting to see how *Assassin's Creed Shadows* continues to perform in the gaming landscape, especially with the upcoming holiday season. Will it maintain its momentum, or will new releases shift the tides? Only time will tell.
