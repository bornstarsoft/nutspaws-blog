Get ready for a wild ride as the highly anticipated urban survival game, Good Luck, is set to launch on PC on April 8, 2026. Developed by the indie studio Happybara Games, this physics-based platformer promises to deliver chaotic gameplay that will keep players on their toes. With its unique blend of humor and challenging mechanics, Good Luck is designed to test your skills as you navigate a city filled with absurd traps and unexpected hazards.

What sets Good Luck apart is its focus on multiplayer chaos, allowing up to five players to join in on the fun. The absence of checkpoints adds an extra layer of intensity, making every jump and climb a nail-biting experience. Whether you're playing solo or teaming up with friends, the unpredictable physics and quirky obstacles ensure that no two playthroughs will be the same.

As we look forward to the release, one can't help but wonder how this game will redefine the urban survival genre. Will you be ready to face the challenges that await in Good Luck?
