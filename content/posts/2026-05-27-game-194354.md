CD Projekt has officially revealed that a third expansion for The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, titled "Songs of the Past," is set to launch in 2027. This exciting announcement marks a significant moment for fans of the franchise, as it promises to return players to the beloved role of Geralt of Rivia for a brand new adventure. Developed in collaboration with Fools Theory, a team of industry veterans, this expansion is highly anticipated by the gaming community.

Scheduled for release on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC, "Songs of the Past" is expected to deliver an engaging narrative and immersive gameplay that fans have come to love. While more details are set to be unveiled in late summer 2026, the announcement has already stirred excitement, especially as it coincides with the tenth anniversary of the fan-favorite expansion, Blood and Wine.

As CD Projekt continues to expand the Witcher universe with multiple projects, including The Witcher 4 and a remake of the original game, one can't help but wonder how "Songs of the Past" will shape the future of this iconic series. What are your expectations for this new expansion?
