Moon Active has just launched Jelly Busters: Puzzle Game, a vibrant arcade-style puzzler that invites players into a colorful skatepark filled with elastic jellies. This game not only challenges your puzzle-solving skills but also offers a unique twist where every shot you take helps unveil a hidden picture beneath stacks of glowing cubes. The combination of soothing ASMR-style feedback and engaging gameplay creates an immersive experience that keeps players coming back for more.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter increasingly complex challenges that require strategic thinking and quick reflexes. Unlockable level boosters and competitive leaderboards add layers of excitement, making each clean run a measurable achievement. Whether you're a casual player or a puzzle enthusiast, Jelly Busters: Puzzle Game promises to deliver both relaxation and thrill in equal measure.

With its captivating visuals and engaging mechanics, this game is set to become a favorite among mobile gamers. Will you join the jelly crew and conquer the colorful skatepark?
