The gaming world is buzzing with excitement as HAOPLAY announces the upcoming release of Quack Quack Attack - PEGG Blaster, a mobile puzzle game that promises to redefine casual gaming on iOS and Android. Players will step into the webbed feet of Captain Quack, navigating a vibrant yet perilous oceanic world filled with monsters, all while managing a unique squad of birds to tackle challenging puzzles.

What sets Quack Quack Attack apart is its innovative gameplay mechanics that blend strategy with fun. Players can choose from up to fifty unique birds, each belonging to one of six classes, allowing for endless combinations and strategies. The game's engaging pachinko-style board offers a fresh twist on the puzzle genre, encouraging players to think critically about their moves as they ricochet eggs to defeat enemies and clear levels.

With pre-registration now open, players can look forward to a treasure trove of rewards upon launch, including exclusive avatars and powerful in-game items.
